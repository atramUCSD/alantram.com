Who is ServiceNow for?

–

Everyone who is digitally transforming their business can use ServiceNow to move faster with AI and automation. The platform and its native workflows serve all business functions—IT, HR, customer service, finance, and procurement—across industries and company sizes. It integrates with legacy systems and newer solutions, becoming the digital backbone of your enterprise. .

What is the ServiceNow AI Platform?

–

The ServiceNow AI Platform is the cloud-based foundation for all ServiceNow products. It connects and automates business workflows across the enterprise using one architecture, one data model, and a collection of AI capabilities, business applications, and low‑code/no‑code tools.

What is an AI agent?

–

An AI agent is an autonomous system designed to gather data, make decisions, and execute tasks to achieve predefined goals. It adapts to new information, learns over time, and can manage a wide range of tasks—from simple repetitive actions to complex problem‑solving. ServiceNow AI Agents automate workflows, streamline operations, and adapt to diverse business needs across IT, HR, CRM, and more.

What is ServiceNow IT Service Management?

–

ServiceNow IT Service Management (ITSM) delivers resilient enterprise IT services, boosts IT productivity, and improves employee experiences—all on the ServiceNow AI Platform.

What is Service Operations?

–

Service Operations combines IT Service Management (ITSM) and IT Operations Management (ITOM) to help IT teams work together to deliver resilient services, boost productivity, and modernize experiences.
